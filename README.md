# COVID-19 Impact on Global Economy — EDA & Trend Analysis

##  Overview
Analysed economic indicators (GDP growth, unemployment, inflation) 
across 20 countries from 2018–2023 to study the impact of COVID-19 
and subsequent recovery patterns.

## Tools & Libraries
Python, Pandas, NumPy, Matplotlib, Seaborn

##  Key Findings
- Global GDP crashed to -5.9% in 2020 (worst since 2008)
- Asia & North America recovered fastest in 2021
- Strong -0.74 correlation between GDP growth and unemployment
- Post-COVID inflation surged to 9% average in 2022
